About Us

Legacy Sugar Company Ltd is a Nigerian agribusiness company focused on large-scale sugarcane cultivation, sugar processing, ethanol production, and power generation. The company is committed to food security, job creation, and sustainable industrial development.

Our Project

  • Large-scale sugarcane plantation
  • Integrated sugar mill
  • Ethanol and renewable power generation
  • Strategic partnerships for expansion across Nigeria

Soil Test & Climate Data

Soil analysis confirms fertile loamy soil suitable for high-yield sugarcane cultivation. The region enjoys favorable rainfall, temperature, and sunshine levels, enabling year-round farming.

Legacy Sugar adopts climate-smart agricultural practices to mitigate climate change and promote sustainability.
